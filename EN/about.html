About Me

Hi! I'm Gabriel and I make videogames. I have a DEC and BAC in videogame developpement.

I am interested in all aspects of videogame creation but more particulary, programmation. I enjoy solving logic puzzles and creating new features for the player to play with.

I prefer fast pace games and enjoy coop more than competitive.

I make games with Unity and Unreal Engine 4/5. I also began to learn Godot for 2D projects. I also know how to use Blender, Maya, Substance Painter and Aseprite to make assets but my artistic side is not my strong point. Other than that, I do a bit of Android and Web developpement, like this website!
