Excess Mana

Tower defense/exploration game - Windows- 2020

Excess Mana is a tower defense exploration game.

This game was created for Wonderjam Autumn 2020, a 48h game jam organised by the University of Quebec at Chicoutimi (UQAC) for the students. I was in a team of 4.

I mainly did art and level design in this game. I did a bit of coding for the spawning of the runes around the map and helped on the mechanic to place towers in the main room.
