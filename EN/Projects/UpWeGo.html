Up We Go

Physic-based plateformer - Windows - 2022


Up We Go is a Third-Person physic-based plateforming game, which can be played with up to 8 players online. The goal is to reach the end by crossing different obstacles by climbing and rolling.

This game was made for my final class at UQAC, in a team of 6. The goal of the class was simply to make a complete game.

I worked on the player character. I made the movement system and its mesh, along with the customizations options. I also worked on the UI, such as the main menu, the pause menu and the end screen.
