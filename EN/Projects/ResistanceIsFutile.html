Resistance is Futile

Turn based procedural RPG - Windows and Browser - 2021

Resistance is Futile is a turn based RPG where you control a group of 3 heroes fighting against endless waves of enemies.

This game was created for Wonderjam Autumn 2021, a 48h game jam organised by the University of Quebec at Chicoutimi (UQAC) for the students. I was in a team of 4.

I worked on the generation of enemy and items stats, as well as the backend for calculating character stats depending on their equipped items.
