Stack The Odds

Jeu de constrution de tour de dés - Windows et Navigateur - 2022


Stack the Odds is a Dice tower building game, where the goal is to make the highest standing tower of dice with different types of dice of random size.

This game was created for the GMTK Gamejam 2022, the biggest gamejam of the year with more than 6000 participants. I was in a team of 3.

I did the main gameplay loop: taking and dropping dices, being able to zoom in and out, auto-adjusting the camera height depending on the current tower height and the laser indicatiing the dice drop position. I also did the code for the UI and loading screen
